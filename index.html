heheautoclicker

A simple auto-clicker utility to automate mouse clicks

Click Settings

Minimum: 1ms, Maximum: 10000ms
Press F8 to toggle start/stop

Instructions:

  • Set your desired click interval in milliseconds
  • Choose which mouse button to click
  • Click "Start Clicking" or press F8 to begin
  • The clicker will click at your current mouse position
  • Press F8 or click "Stop Clicking" to stop
